# Subjective Wellbeing Among Youth

This repository contains the LaTeX manuscript, quantitative and qualitative data analaysis for a research study "Beyond Objective Measures: Subjective Wellbeing and Life Satisfaction of Youth in Chandigarh" examining subjective wellbeing (PWI-A) and objective wellbeing (YDI) among youth in Chandigarh, India. 

## Research questions
RQ1: What is the overall state of objective wellbeing among youth of Chandigarh based on Youth Development Index?
RQ2: What is the overall state of subjective wellbeing among youth of Chandigarh based on Personal Wellbeing Index? 
RQ3: What are the major facilitators that enhance subjective wellbeing of youth in Chandigarh? 
RQ4: What are the major inhibitors that negatively affect the subjective wellbeing of youth in Chandigarh?  

## Methods
- Stratified random sampling (N = 60)
- Personal Wellbeing Index - Adult (PWI-A)
- Youth Development Index (YDI)

## Repository Structure
- `main.tex` - LaTeX manuscript source (excerpt)
- `final.pdf` - Compiled manuscript (excerpt)
- `qualitative/PWI themebook .md` - Thematic coding
