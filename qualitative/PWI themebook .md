## Overview
This theemebook documents the  deductive thematic analysis combined with score-based clustering

Coding was conducted using an deductive thematic score-based clustering approach:
- Deductive categories guided by PWI-A index domains
- Thematic categories arranged based on facilitator and inhibitors
- Score based clustering (extreme case analysis) based of score thershold 0-4 and 7-10 

## Theme 1: Standard of Living

### Theme 1.1: Inhibitors (0-4)
Financial dependence on parents, poor living conditions, economic constraint 

### Theme 1.2: Facilitator (7-10)
Basic Needs fulfillment, City space, Education attainment, Financial and material support by family, financial freedom Gratitude, Positive living conditions, Social Support 


## Theme 2: Health 
### Theme 2.1: Inhibitors (0-4)
Frequent illness, long term illness, Obesity, Poor diet, Poor Sleep, Reproductive health issue, Time poverty 

### Theme 2.2: Facilitator (7-10)
Absence of disease/illness, City health facilities, Emotion support, Healthy diet, Meditation/Art, Physical activity, Physical fitness, young age 


##Theme 3: Achieving  

### Theme 3.1: Inhibitors (0-4)
Bribery and corruption, Ineffective educational outcomes, Economic constraints, Lack of clarity, Peer pressure, Perceived discrimination, Regret and self-blame, Social disconnect, Unemployment

### Theme 3.2: Facilitator (7-10)
Achievement and recognitions, educational attainment, financial freedom, meaningful work, Resilience and learning, Skills and Exposure, Social capital, Social Support, Stable job


##Theme 4: Relationship 

### Theme 4.1: Inhibitors (0-4)
Lack of trust, Time scarcity, Emotional distance  

### Theme 4.2: Facilitator (7-10)
Attachment & Belonging, Communication skills, Emotional support, loyalty and reciprocity, Relational Selectivity, Social abilities, Strong family bond, Trust and understanding 

#Theme 5: Safety 

### Theme 5.1: Inhibitors (0-4)
Perceived Absence of Absolute Safety, Gender-based Vulnerability, Distrust in Institutions, Gender-based Vulnerability

### Theme 5.2: Facilitator (7-10)
Self-efficacy & Physical Strength, Conflict Avoidance, Family & Partner Protection, Infrastructure & Mobility Personal Vigilance, , Supportive Social Networks, Urban Governance & Policing


#Theme 6: Community 

### Theme 6.1: Inhibitors (0-4)
Loss of Emotional Community, Weak Identification with Community

### Theme 6.2: Facilitator (7-10)
Absence of Discrimination/Liberal norms, Community Unity, Cultural Pride & Identity, Joint Family Structures, Learning & Guidance, Positive Social Interactions, Supportive Social Environment 

#Theme 7: Future 

### Theme 7.1: Inhibitors (0-4)
Economic Instability, Educational & Skill Deficits, Employment Uncertainty, Life-cycle Pressures, Psychological Barriers, Time and Role Constraints

### Theme 7.2: Facilitator (7-10)
Clear Goals & Planning, Educational Progress, Employment security, Family Support, Positive Life Transitions, Self-confidence & Optimism, Skill Confidence, Spiritual or Moral Belief
